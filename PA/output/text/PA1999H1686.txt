29     (a)  General rule.--Upon timely receipt of notification of
30  removal under section 901(b), the registration commission shall
1  proceed as follows:
2         (1)  The signature on the notification document shall be
3     compared with the signature of the elector as it appears on
4     file with the commission.
5         (2)  If the signature appears authentic, the commission
6     shall enter the change of residence on the registration card
7     of the elector in the general register and district register
8     and shall transfer the registration card of the elector from
9     the district register of the election district of previous
10     residence to the district register of the election district
11     of new residence.
12         (3)  If a request for transfer which is determined to be
13     authentic under paragraph (2) shows a removal within the
14     period of 30 days preceding an election, the commission,
15     after such election, shall enter the change of residence on
16     the registration card of the elector in the general register
17     and district register and shall transfer the registration
18     card of the elector from the district register of the
19     election district of previous residence. The commission shall
20     advise the elector promptly in writing of its action.
21         (4)  When an elector has filed with the commission a
22     notice that the elector has moved from that county to
23     another, if the signature appears authentic, the commission
24     shall enter the change of residence on the registration card
25     of the elector in the general register and district register
26     and shall transfer the registration card of the elector from
27     the district register of the election district of last
28     residence to the commission of the county of the elector's
29     new residence.
30         (5)  If a request for transfer which is determined to be
1     authentic under paragraph (4) shows a removal within the
2     period of 30 days preceding an election, the commission of
3     the former county of the elector, after such election, shall
4     enter the change of residence on the registration card of the
5     elector in the general register and district register and
6     shall transfer the registration card of the elector from the
7     district register of the election district of previous
8     residence to the election commission of the elector's new
9     county of residence. The commission of the elector's former
10     county shall advise the elector promptly in writing of its
11     action.

16  Section 904.  Physical disability.
17     (a)  Eligibility.--If an elector by reason of blindness,
18  disability or inability to read or write, is unable to read the
19  names on the ballot or on the voting machine labels, or is
20  unable to see or mark the ballot or operate the voting machine
21  or to enter the voting compartment or voting machine booth
22  without assistance, the following apply:
23         (1)  The elector may, at least ten days prior to the next
24     election, personally make application to the commission or a
25     registrar or a clerk.
26         (2)  The application must request the entry of the exact
27     nature of the disability on the elector's registration card.
28         (3)  The commission shall make the entry as appropriate.
29         (4)  If the disability is not entered on the registration
30     card, the elector may receive assistance if the elector
1     completes a declaration in the polling place.
2         (5)  Following the election, the commission shall examine
3     declarations completed under this section, and shall update
4     the elector's voter registration record to reflect the need
5     for assistance and the nature of the disability.
